🌊 Top Tourist Places in Vizag

🌳 Central Park

A beautiful city park perfect for walking, relaxation, and family outings.

🏖 RK Beach

The most famous beach in Vizag known for its sunrise views and lively atmosphere.

🚢 INS Kursura Submarine Museum

A retired Indian Navy submarine converted into a unique museum.

✈️ TU-142 Aircraft Museum

A naval aircraft museum showcasing India's maritime aviation history.

🚁 UH-3H Helicopter Museum

A preserved naval helicopter displayed for visitors near the beach road.

🛩 Sea Harrier Museum

Home to the legendary Sea Harrier fighter aircraft used by the Indian Navy.

🏛 Visakha Museum

Features historical artifacts, photographs, and cultural exhibits of Vizag.

⛪ Ross Hill

A scenic hilltop church offering panoramic views of the city and harbor.

🌊 Port Promenade

A beautiful coastal walkway with sea views, museums, and attractions.

⛰ Kailasagiri

A hilltop tourist destination with ropeway rides, glass bridge and stunning ocean views.

🌉 Glass Bridge

A thrilling glass bridge at Kailasagiri offering spectacular scenery.

🔱 Trishul View Point

A scenic viewpoint known for breathtaking views of the city.

🦁 Indira Gandhi Zoological Park

One of India's largest zoos with diverse wildlife and lush greenery.

🌿 Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary

A protected forest area ideal for nature lovers and trekking enthusiasts.

🏄 Rushikonda Beach

Popular for water sports, golden sands, and crystal-clear waters.

🛕 Thotlakonda

An ancient Buddhist complex located on a hill overlooking the sea.

🙏 Simhachalam Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha attracting devotees year-round.

🏝 Bheemunipatnam Beach

A peaceful beach town with historical Dutch-era landmarks.

🌅 Dolphin's Nose Lighthouse

A landmark lighthouse offering breathtaking views of the coastline.

🏖 Yarada Beach

A clean and serene beach surrounded by hills and natural beauty.
